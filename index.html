Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

In the New Bangladesh, the fusion of idea, innovation, and vision is propelling the nation towards unprecedented growth. A relentless pursuit of progress fuels technological advancements, sustainable development, and socio-economic transformation. Visionary leaders and empowered citizens are redefining the future, focusing on education, digitalization, and infrastructure. However, challenges like inequality, resource management, and climate change remain. Overcoming these hurdles requires collective resilience, adaptive policies, and global collaboration. With creativity and determination, New Bangladesh stands poised to carve a path of inclusive prosperity, where innovation meets the aspirations of its people for a brighter tomorrow.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
